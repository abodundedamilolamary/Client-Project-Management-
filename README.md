## ADM Styles — Client Project Management

## Overview
A real-world project management case study from ADM Styles, a fashion design business. This documents how professional PM methodologies were applied to manage client orders, timelines, resources and expectations.

## My Role
Fashion Designer & Project Manager | ADM Styles | Owner

## Case Study — 
Custom Female Wears Delivery

## Project Brief
Deliverable: 7 sets of custom female wears
Original Timeline: 2 weeks
Client Request: Early delivery within 1 week

## Challenges
Client requested scope change mid-project
Shortened timeline threatened quality and cost

## What I Did
Communicated transparently with client on timeline impact
Negotiated budget increase to accommodate request
Hired additional resource to meet new deadline
Successfully delivered on client's requested date

## Key Outcome
Delivered all 7 pieces on time
Maintained client satisfaction through clear communication
Demonstrated real-world scope and budget management

## Skills Demonstrated
Scope Management | Stakeholder Communication | Resource Management | Budget Negotiation | Risk Handling
